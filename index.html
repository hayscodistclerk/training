Organization


Rules


  1. Employees are to abide by the human resources personel policy.
  2. Employees are to be respectful and modest.
  3. There are two divisions (criminal and civil) . Each division has a direct supervisor in charge.
  4. The direct supervisor is the supervisor of whatever division you are in.
  5. Supervisors have direct power over their division and can organize said division however they wish
  6. Employees are to faithfully undertake the tasks their supervisor assigns to them
  7. Employees are given priority within the office to fill vacant positions as a promotion before any other candidate outside of our department unless otherwise specified by the department head.
  8. The employees outside of our department that work for the county are given second priority unless otherwise specified by the department head.

Supervisor Responsibilities


  1. A supervisor is to organise their division as they see fit.
  2. A supervisor is to promote good morale in the office.
  3. A supervisor is to promote efficiency in the office.
  4. A supervisor is to post jobs for vacancies in their division
  5. A supervisor is to keep timesheets for employees in their division and take over timesheets should another division supervisor be vacant.
  6. A supervisor is to conduct interviews when there is no one or no one willing in prime or secondary priority to be promoted to a vacant position.
  7. A supervisor is to procure items for their division as needed and or as they become scarce. First asking the Assistant Department head and secondly the department head.
